Bhavani Island

Bhavani Island situated in the midst of the Krishna River, at Vijayawada. It is located at the upstream of Prakasam Barrage and is considered as one of the largest river islands in India, with an area of 133 acres.The primary entry point to Bhavani Island is Berm Park, which is on the Vijayawada-Hyderabad Highway.Andhra Pradesh Tourism runs a couple of hotels here - one at Berm Park and the other-on Bhavani Island.
